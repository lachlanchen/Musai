# Source And Cleaned Seed

## Source Seed

```text
你是一只猪

你是一只小小猪
怎么那么快乐
吃得那么多，却还是很饿
小小猪，我想做只小小猪

没有作业，没有工作
没有论文，没有考核
我是一只小小猪
小小猪

好想待在被窝
听你唱歌
被抚摸
不用工作

哦哦哦，小小猪
```

## Cleaned Direction

Keep:

- the nursery-rhyme simplicity;
- the funny directness of `你是一只猪`;
- the tired-person fantasy of no homework, no work, no paper, no assessment;
- the warm blanket, singing, and being comforted;
- the repeated hook `小小猪`.

Improve:

- stronger chorus memory;
- more rhyme/vowel echo;
- a clearer emotional arc from cute joke to adult pressure relief;
- fewer crowded lines for better music generation.
