# Producer Brief

## Title

```text
你是一只猪
```

Localized titles:

```text
English: You Little Piggy
Japanese: きみはこぶた
```

## One-Line Promise

A cute comfort song for exhausted people who want, just for tonight, to become
a tiny happy pig with no homework, no work, no papers, and no assessments.

## Producer Prompt

```text
Joyful Chinese bedroom pop and cute indie folk song, slightly whiny but warm,
catchy repeated hook, ukulele or soft acoustic guitar, light piano, gentle drums,
warm bass, tiny handclaps, playful ooh-ooh backing vocals, 104 BPM, G major or A major,
4/4, comforting and funny but emotionally resonant. Clear upfront Mandarin
Chinese vocal, cute lazy phrasing, natural pronunciation, connected full-song
melody, no clipped final words, no buried vocal, no real-singer imitation.
Leave musical space; use held vowels and rests; do not cram the melody.
```

## Negative Prompt / Guardrails

```text
Do not make it a children-only song.
Do not make the vocal robotic, shouted, buried, or overly cute.
Do not imitate a real singer.
Do not rush the lyric.
Do not cut off the last word of lines.
Do not overproduce; keep it warm and human.
```

## Suggested Generation Settings

```text
Master language: zh
Duration: 80-95 seconds
BPM: 104
Key: G major in prompt; A major is acceptable if the model naturally lands there
Time signature: 4/4
Seed policy: try 2-3 seeds if first vocal is unclear
Primary route: ACE-Step full song
Review route: Musia quality check + run_pipeline analysis + manual listening
Companion route: master-companion after Chinese master is selected
```
