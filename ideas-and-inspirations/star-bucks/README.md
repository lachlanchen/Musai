# Star Bucks

English-first song idea inspired by the emotional spirit of Mary Oliver's
`Wild Geese` / `野雁`: nature does not judge the traveler; it calls him back
into belonging. This is not a translation or quotation of the poem.

Core image:

- a man traveling alone at sea;
- he speaks with wind, waves, moon, stars, and night;
- he sees magical bucks/deer swimming across the dark water;
- the bucks become a sign of hope and endurance;
- the song says: keep moving, do not give up.

Title meaning:

`Star Bucks` means star-lit bucks, deer of the sea and sky. It should be
treated as a poetic image, not as the coffee brand.

Generation direction:

- language: English-first, with optional small Chinese/Japanese color in the hook;
- style: cinematic ocean folk-pop / indie ballad;
- mood: vast, hopeful, lonely but not defeated;
- vocal: clear emotional male or warm androgynous vocal, upfront and human;
- tempo: 78-84 BPM;
- arrangement: piano, soft acoustic guitar, low drums, oceanic pads, strings in the final chorus;
- avoid: brand jokes, coffee references, real-singer imitation, crowded lyrics.
