# Producer Brief

## Emotional Arc

Lonely departure -> conversation with sea and stars -> vision of star-lit bucks
swimming through the ocean -> renewed courage -> quiet victory.

## Lyric Quality Notes

- Keep English as the main singing language.
- Preserve space between images; do not cram the verse.
- The chorus should be memorable and simple.
- The phrase `Star bucks` should sound magical, not comic.
- Chinese/Japanese words may appear only as color in a companion version.
