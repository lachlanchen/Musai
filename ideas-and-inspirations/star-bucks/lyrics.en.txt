# Star Bucks

[Verse 1]
I left the harbor late at night
With salt upon my hands
The moon was writing silver lines
Across the breathing sand

I asked the waves, "Am I too small
To cross this dark alone?"
They answered with a rolling heart
And carried me from home

[Pre-Chorus]
The wind said, keep your lantern high
The tide said, do not turn
The stars leaned close above my sail
Like quiet fires that burn

[Chorus]
Star bucks, star bucks
Running through the sea
Hooves of light on midnight waves
Still swimming next to me

Star bucks, star bucks
Do not disappear
If you can cross the blackest water
I can make it here

[Verse 2]
I saw their antlers catch the sky
Like branches full of flame
They rose and fell through moonlit foam
And never spoke my name

But every splash became a word
The ocean understood
"You are not lost, you are alive
Keep going while you could"

[Pre-Chorus]
The night said, lay your sorrow down
The rain said, let it pass
The dawn was still beyond the world
But glowing under glass

[Chorus]
Star bucks, star bucks
Running through the sea
Hooves of light on midnight waves
Still swimming next to me

Star bucks, star bucks
Do not disappear
If you can cross the blackest water
I can make it here

[Bridge]
I do not need to be unbroken
I only need to breathe
The wild sky keeps calling softly
Come back, belong, believe

No shore is close, no map is clear
But hope is not a shore
It is the leap inside the dark
That carries me once more

[Final Chorus]
Star bucks, star bucks
Running through the sea
Hooves of light on midnight waves
Still swimming next to me

Star bucks, star bucks
Carry on, my dear
If you can cross the blackest water
I can make it here

[Outro]
The stars are speaking in the foam
The sea is wide and near
I sail with bucks of light tonight
And I will make it here
